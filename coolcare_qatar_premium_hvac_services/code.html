HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency AC failure?

expand_more
Our emergency service fleets are stationed across Doha, Lusail, and Al Wakrah. We guarantee response times within 30 to 45 minutes for emergency breakdowns during peak summer periods, 24/7.

What is included in your Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

expand_more
Our standard AMC includes 3 or 4 preventative service visits per year. This covers deep coil pressure washing, filter cleaning, electrical checks, gas top-ups, drain pan flushings, and emergency callouts with prioritized booking.

Do you install brand-new air conditioning systems?

expand_more
Yes, we provide full AC system design, heat load calculations, and authorized factory installation for Split, Ducted, and VRF systems from premium brands like Daikin, Carrier, Samsung, and O General, complete with full warranties.

Are your HVAC technicians certified and insured?

expand_more
Absolutely. Every CoolCare technician undergoes advanced Gulf-climate specialized technical training, holds valid international HVAC licensing certifications, and is fully insured for residential and commercial sites.
