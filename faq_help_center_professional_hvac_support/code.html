handyman Services & Maintenance Plans

Our standard AMC includes 3 or 4 preventative service visits per year. This covers deep coil pressure washing, filter cleaning, electrical and drainage audits, refrigerant pressure checks, gas top-ups, and prioritised emergency callout booking with discounted spare parts.
Due to the extreme summer temperatures and high ambient dust levels in the Gulf, we recommend professional servicing at least 3-4 times a year. Regular maintenance prevents unexpected breakdowns, maintains energy efficiency, and ensures clean indoor air quality.
Yes, we do. We use state-of-the-art Rotobrush equipment to remove mold, dust, sand, and allergens from your air ducts. We complete the service with EPA-approved, non-toxic sanitizing foggers that eliminate bacteria and keep your air clean and healthy.

emergency Emergency & Repair Services

An HVAC emergency includes a complete cooling failure in Qatari peak summer heat, severe water leaks that threaten ceiling damage, unusual burning odors, or loud metal grinding noises from your AC system. If you experience these, call our 24/7 hotline instantly.
We operate emergency response fleets strategically placed throughout Doha, Lusail, and Al Wakrah. For urgent emergencies, we guarantee dispatch arrival within 30 to 60 minutes of your call to restore safety and comfort.
